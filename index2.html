HexaBot Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how HexaBot ("HexaBot," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the HexaBot moderation bot ("Service").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide: When you interact with HexaBot, we may collect information that you provide directly, such as commands you execute, messages you send, and settings you configure.

1.2 Usage Information: We may collect information about your use of HexaBot, including your interactions with the Service, settings preferences, and usage patterns.

2. How We Use Information

2.1 Providing the Service: We use the information we collect to operate, maintain, and improve HexaBot, including providing features, personalizing your experience, and troubleshooting issues.

2.2 Communication: We may use your contact information to communicate with you about HexaBot, such as responding to inquiries, providing support, and sending important updates or announcements.

2.3 Analytics: We may use information collected through HexaBot for analytics purposes, such as analyzing usage trends, monitoring performance, and improving the Service.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 Third-Party Service Providers: We may share information with third-party service providers that assist us in operating, maintaining, and improving HexaBot. These service providers are authorized to use your information only as necessary to provide services to us.

3.2 Legal Compliance: We may disclose information in response to a legal request or obligation, such as complying with a subpoena, court order, or other legal process, or to enforce our rights or protect the security or integrity of HexaBot.

3.3 Consent: We may share information with your consent or at your direction.

4. Data Retention

We retain information collected through HexaBot for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the security of information collected through HexaBot against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6. Children's Privacy

HexaBot is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the revised Privacy Policy on the HexaBot website. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates.
